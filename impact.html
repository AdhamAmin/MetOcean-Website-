Case Study

North Sea Wind Farm

The Challenge

A major energy developer needed accurate 50-year return period wave data to design turbine foundations for a new wind farm in the North Sea.

Our Solution

MetOcean deployed two waverider buoys for real-time validation and ran a high-resolution 30-year hindcast model. We provided a comprehensive design criteria report compliant with IEC 61400-3.

The Result

The project was approved with optimized foundation designs, saving the client $2.5M in material costs while ensuring safety standards were met.

Case Studies

Offshore Wind Farm: Site & Impact

Challenge: A renewable energy firm needed to site a 500MW offshore wind farm while minimizing impact on marine mammals and fisheries.

Solution: Our team provided end-to-end consulting, from high-resolution geophysical surveys and habitat mapping to advanced acoustic monitoring and stakeholder engagement.

Results: A-level site certification achieved with a 90% reduction in predicted marine mammal interactions, gaining full regulatory approval 6 months ahead of schedule.

Aquaculture Circular Economy

Challenge: A large salmon aquaculture company sought to eliminate waste and achieve a 'circular' model.

Solution: OceanSustain designed and implemented an integrated multi-trophic aquaculture (IMTA) system, using seaweed and shellfish to capture and repurpose waste streams.

Results: 95% waste nutrient recapture, $1.2M in new revenue from secondary crops (seaweed), and a 3rd-party 'Sustainable' certification achieved.
